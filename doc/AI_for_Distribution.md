# AI Basics for Distribution Centre Planning & Management

Focusing on the strategic and operational layers of running a high-performing DC, balanced with safety and compliance.

---

## LEVEL 1: SLOT-PLAN DRAFTING
**Goal:** Optimize product placement for faster picking and reduced travel time.
* **USE IT FOR:** Heat-mapping fast-movers (ABC analysis), seasonal re-slotting plans.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Draft a re-slotting strategy for our [Season] period. Based on [Data], identify the top 10% of high-velocity SKUs and suggest optimal placement near the packing stations."

---

## LEVEL 2: LABOR & SAFETY DYNAMICS
**Goal:** Align staffing levels with fluctuating volumes while preventing fatigue.
* **USE IT FOR:** Shift scheduling, productivity benchmarking, fatigue zone identification.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Calculate the required headcount for receiving and picking teams for [Volume]. Include mandatory rest intervals and suggest a rotation plan for heavy-lifting zones."

---

## LEVEL 3: SPACE & HAZARD AUDIT
**Goal:** Maximize the cube and floor footprint while maintaining clear safety zones.
* **USE IT FOR:** Racking configuration analysis, aisle width optimization, identifying obstructions.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Review our current warehouse layout. Ensure all racking complies with standards and confirm that clear zones are maintained around all fire suppression points: [paste layout specs]."

---

## LEVEL 4: SAFETY AUDIT & COMPLIANCE
**Goal:** Proactively identify risks before accidents or audit failures occur.
* **USE IT FOR:** Analyzing MHE (Material Handling Equipment) near-miss logs, PPE compliance checks, safety checklists.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Analyze these MHE incident logs. Identify the top 3 'hot zones' for forklift/pedestrian near-misses and suggest layout changes or sensor placements: [paste logs]."

---

## LEVEL 5: THROUGHPUT & BOTTLENECK ANALYSIS
**Goal:** Identify and clear "choke points" in the flow of goods.
* **USE IT FOR:** Conveyor speed balancing, packing station efficiency, dock-to-stock time reduction.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Analyze our 'Dock-to-Stock' process. Current average time is [X] hours. Identify where delays are most likely to occur and suggest AI-driven automation to cut this time by 30%."

---

## LEVEL 6: INVENTORY RECONCILIATION
**Goal:** Maintain near-perfect cycle count accuracy without stopping operations.
* **USE IT FOR:** Shrinkage analysis, cycle count scheduling, root-cause analysis for discrepancies.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Summarize our last three cycle count reports. Highlight the SKUs with the highest variance and suggest if the issue is due to receiving errors or picking errors: [paste data]."

---

## LEVEL 7: DISRUPTION & EMERGENCY SIMULATION
**Goal:** Prepare for equipment failure or facility emergencies.
* **USE IT FOR:** Sortation system failure scenarios, power outages, emergency evacuation modeling.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Our main sortation conveyor is down for 6 hours. Create a contingency plan for manual routing and prioritize which orders should be processed first while maintaining floor safety."

---

## LEVEL 8: AUTOMATION & SAFETY TECH SCOUTING
**Goal:** Evaluate high-tech solutions for productivity and injury prevention.
* **USE IT FOR:** Exoskeletons for picking, AI-vision safety monitoring, ASRS viability.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Provide a comparison of [Tech A] vs. [Tech B]. Focus on implementation time, flexibility for SKU changes, and the 'Break-even' point including reduced accident costs."
