# AI Basics for Freight Forwarding

Focusing on global multi-modal transport, customs compliance, and proactive quote management.

---

## LEVEL 1: BOOKING & DOCUMENT DRAFTING
**Goal:** Automate the heavy lifting of repetitive shipping paperwork.
* **USE IT FOR:** Bills of Lading (BOL), commercial invoices, arrival notices.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Generate a draft Bill of Lading based on the following packing list. Ensure the Incoterms are set to [CIF/FOB] and the 'Notify Party' is [Company Name]: [paste data]."

---

## LEVEL 2: SPOT-RATE QUOTING
**Goal:** Respond to client inquiries in minutes rather than hours.
* **USE IT FOR:** Standardizing markups, validating carrier spot rates, quote comparisons.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Create a freight quote for a [20ft/40ft] container from [Origin] to [Destination]. Compare current spot rates from [Carrier A] and [Carrier B], apply our [X%] margin, and list surcharges."

---

## LEVEL 3: CUSTOMS COMPLIANCE AUDIT
**Goal:** Prevent costly fines and shipment seizures.
* **USE IT FOR:** HS Code classification verification, sanction checks, import/export restrictions.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Review this list of goods for a shipment to [Country]. Verify the suggested HS Codes and flag any items that may require additional permits or are subject to anti-dumping duties."

---

## LEVEL 4: ROUTE & MODAL OPTIMIZATION
**Goal:** Balance cost, speed, and carbon footprint dynamically.
* **USE IT FOR:** Sea-to-Air hybrid routing, port congestion analysis, CO2 emission calculations.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Cargo needs to be in [City] by [Date]. Sea freight ETAs are too late. Suggest a multi-modal (Sea-Air) route via [Transit Hub] that meets the deadline with lowest cost increase."

---

## LEVEL 5: EMAIL & SHIPMENT SYNTHESIS
**Goal:** Extract critical shipment updates from thousands of daily emails.
* **USE IT FOR:** Summarizing carrier delay notices, identifying action items from vendor threads.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Summarize this email thread regarding shipment [Ref #]. Identify the root cause of the delay, the new ETA, and draft a proactive update email to the client."

---

## LEVEL 6: CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE & VISIBILITY
**Goal:** Move from reactive "where is my cargo?" calls to proactive reporting.
* **USE IT FOR:** Weekly executive shipment summaries, milestone alerts, delay impact reports.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Write a weekly shipment status report for [Client Name]. Highlight any exceptions, provide a summary of on-time performance, and lead with high-priority project cargo status."

---

## LEVEL 7: EXCEPTION STRESS-TESTING
**Goal:** Develop "Plan B" strategies for strikes, weather, or blank sailings.
* **USE IT FOR:** Port strike contingency planning, rerouting scenarios, capacity surge planning.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Act as a Trade Lane Manager. If [Port] goes on strike next week, what are our top 3 alternative gateway options for cargo currently on the water? Rank by transit time and cost."

---

## LEVEL 8: CARRIER & MARKET SCOUTING
**Goal:** Build a resilient carrier network and identify emerging trade trends.
* **USE IT FOR:** Evaluating niche carriers, tracking market rate trends (SCFI/FBX), container availability research.
* **PROMPT TEMPLATE:** "Research the current market concentration on the [Lane] for 2026. Identify which carriers have added the most capacity recently and any emerging risks regarding container availability."
